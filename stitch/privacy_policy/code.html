Data Collection

We collect information only when it is strictly necessary to provide our services, ensure the safety of our caseload, or process donations. This includes:

Direct Information

Names, contact details, and payment information provided during portal login or donation processes.

Automated Data

Technical logs, IP addresses (anonymized), and device information for security monitoring and analytics.

The Radiant Guardian Security Standard

"The safety of a survivor begins with the safety of their data."

Zero-Knowledge Storage

Sensitive case recordings and logs are encrypted with keys that Lucero staff cannot access without explicit authorization.

Geofencing Protection

Location data for visits is metadata-stripped and only stored as high-level regional indicators to protect specific shelters.

Audited Access

Every single interaction with sensitive data is logged and reviewed weekly by our internal compliance team.

Your Rights under GDPR

Right to Erasure

You may request the permanent deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

Data Portability

Request a machine-readable export of all information you have provided to our portal.

Right to Rectification

Quickly correct any inaccuracies in your donor or staff profile via the settings panel.

Withdraw Consent

Opt-out of non-essential communications or data processing with a single click.
